About Me

Coding

I fell into the mechanical keyboard hobby a few years ago. As I built smaller and smaller boards, I learned how to modify their programming. Split, 40% keyboards with tactile switches are my favorite! I found that I enjoyed the process of coding and had an interest in design, so I eventually began my journey into web development. Fun fact: I like the color green.

Hobbies

I love music! Listening, playing, singing, etc. I played cello in high school, and I have been playing bass guitar (Yamaha BB234) since early 2022. I enjoy drawing, knitting, and crocheting. My favorite book genres are science fiction, fantasy, and speculative fiction.
